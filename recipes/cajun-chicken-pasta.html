Cajun Chicken Pasta

Description

Easy to make and delicious to eat, this cajun chicken pasta recipe includes bell peppers and mushrooms to spice up this kitchen classic!

Estimated time to make: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces linguine (or other pasta)
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 teaspoons of cajun seasoning
  • 2 peppers (red and/or green, sliced)
  • 4 fresh mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 green onion (chopped)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of lemon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
